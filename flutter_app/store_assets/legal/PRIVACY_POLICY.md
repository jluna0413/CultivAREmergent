# CultivAREmergant Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** November 3, 2025  
**Last Updated:** November 3, 2025

## 1. Introduction

CultivAREmergant ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our cannabis cultivation management application ("CultivAREmergant App") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

**IMPORTANT:** The CultivAREmergant App is designed for legal cannabis cultivation and educational purposes only. Users must comply with all applicable local, state, and federal laws regarding cannabis cultivation and use.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, username, password
- **Profile Data:** Profile picture, cultivation preferences, experience level
- **Communication Data:** Messages, feedback, support requests

### 2.2 Cultivation Data
- **Plant Information:** Strain names, growth stages, harvest dates, yields
- **Environmental Data:** Temperature, humidity, pH levels, lighting schedules
- **Growth Tracking:** Photos, notes, progress logs, cultivation metrics
- **Harvest Records:** Batch information, processing details, testing results

### 2.3 Device and Usage Information
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **App Usage:** Features used, session duration, crash reports
- **Location Data:** GPS coordinates for environmental monitoring (with consent)
- **Sensor Data:** IoT device connections, sensor readings, automation settings

### 2.4 Automatically Collected Information
- **Technical Data:** IP address, browser type, app version, performance metrics
- **Cookies and Tracking:** Local storage, session data, analytics tracking

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Primary Purposes
- **Cultivation Management:** Track plant growth, environmental conditions, and harvest records
- **Personalization:** Customize app experience based on cultivation preferences
- **Communication:** Send important updates, alerts, and support responses
- **Analytics:** Improve app performance and user experience

### 3.2 Legal Compliance
- **Age Verification:** Confirm users meet minimum age requirements for cannabis cultivation
- **Jurisdictional Compliance:** Ensure use complies with local cannabis laws
- **Record Keeping:** Maintain cultivation records for legal compliance
- **Reporting:** Provide data for regulatory requirements where legally mandated

### 3.3 Marketing and Communications
- **Product Updates:** Notify about new features, cultivation tips, and educational content
- **Educational Content:** Send cultivation guides, best practices, and industry news
- **Promotional Communications:** Share special offers and partnership opportunities (with consent)

## 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 We Do Not Sell Personal Information

### 4.2 Limited Sharing Circumstances
- **Service Providers:** Third-party services that help us operate the app (cloud storage, analytics, customer support)
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law, court order, or legal process
